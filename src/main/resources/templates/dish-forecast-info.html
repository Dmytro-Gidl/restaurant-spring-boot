Forecasting models

This page explains the forecasting options for dishes.

  • Holt‑Winters: triple exponential smoothing capturing level, trend and seasonality. Works well for steady demand with gradual changes.
  • ARIMA(1,0,0): a simple autoregressive model using the previous value to project the next. Suitable for short histories without seasonality.
  • Auto‑ARIMA: searches a range of ARIMA parameters and selects the best according to AIC.
