Forecasting models

This page explains the dish forecast options in everyday language.

  • Holt‑Winters: follows the recent trend and smooths out bumps. It is the default choice and works well when demand changes gradually.
  • ARIMA (1,0,0): looks mostly at the last month to guess the next. Use it when numbers swing up and down around an average.
  • Auto‑ARIMA: tries a couple of simple patterns and picks the one that fits recent data best.

If only a few scattered months of orders exist, all models may show the same flat line. More completed orders are needed to see real differences.
