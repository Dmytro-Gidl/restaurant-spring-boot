Forecast guide

Use these notes to choose a forecasting method. The examples show how each option behaves.

  • Holt‑Winters follows gradual trends. Example: monthly sales of 5, 10, 15 will continue upward.
  • ARIMA (1,0,0) reacts quickly to recent swings. Example: alternating 20 and 5 is tracked closely.
  • Auto‑ARIMA tests a few simple patterns and keeps the one that fits best. Sparse data often yields a flat line.

With only a handful of completed months, all models may show the same flat forecast. Add more finished orders for clearer differences.

Sample datasets for experimentation are available in docs/forecast-scenarios.sql.
